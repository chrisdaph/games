Toddler Games for Good Behavior and Big Feelings

Toddler Games is a small collection of free activities made for ages 2 to 5. Each one turns an everyday skill, like waiting your turn or noticing how you feel, into a simple, friendly game that plays right in the browser.

Games in This Collection

  • Stop and Go, tap only when the light turns green, a gentle way to practice waiting.
  • Feelings Match, match a face to how it feels, building emotional vocabulary.
  • Balloon Breathing, slow breaths grow and shrink a friendly balloon, a simple calm down tool.
  • Good Choice, pick the kind response in short, everyday scenarios.

Why Play These Games?

Young children learn self-control and emotional skills best through short, repeatable, low-pressure play. These games have no timers, no losing, and no scary sounds, just gentle encouragement so children can practice waiting, naming feelings, calming down, and choosing kindness at their own pace.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these toddler games free?

Yes. Every game is free to play in your browser with no download, sign-up, or payment.

What skills do these games teach?

Waiting and impulse control, naming and understanding feelings, calming breathing, and making kind choices in everyday situations.

What age are these games for?

They are designed for toddlers and preschoolers, roughly ages 2 to 5, though any age can enjoy them.
