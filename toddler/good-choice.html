About Good Choice

Good Choice walks toddlers through six short, everyday scenarios, a shared toy, a spilled drink, a sad friend, and asks them to pick a response. Every answer gets warm, encouraging feedback, so children see and hear what a kind choice sounds like without ever feeling scolded.

How to Play

  1. Read the short scenario and look at the picture.
  2. Tap the response you would choose.
  3. See friendly feedback about that choice.
  4. Move through all six scenarios to finish the game!

Why Practicing Choices Helps

Young children learn behavior expectations best through repetition in low-stakes, playful settings. Walking through common moments, like waiting a turn or comforting a friend, ahead of time helps children recognize and choose the kind response more easily when a real moment like it comes along.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you play Good Choice?

Read the short scenario, then tap the response you would pick. Each answer gives friendly feedback before moving to the next scenario.

What does Good Choice teach?

It walks children through small, everyday moments, sharing, waiting, honesty, kindness, and models a good response for each one.

Is Good Choice free to play?

Yes. It plays free in any web browser with nothing to download or install.
