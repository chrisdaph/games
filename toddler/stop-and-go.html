About Stop and Go

Stop and Go is a simple game that helps toddlers practice one of the hardest early skills, pausing before acting. A big light flips between red and green at a friendly pace. When it turns green, tap the button. When it is red, the game gently asks you to wait, no scolding, no losing, just another chance a moment later.

How to Play

  1. Watch the big light at the top.
  2. When it turns green, tap the GO button.
  3. If it is still red, wait, the light will turn green soon.
  4. Collect six stars by tapping on green each time!

Why This Helps

Waiting for a signal before acting is an early form of impulse control, the same skill children use when waiting for a turn, staying calm before answering, or pausing before reacting to a big feeling. Short, playful practice like this builds that skill gently, without pressure.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you play Stop and Go?

Watch the light. Tap the button only when it turns green. If you tap on red, just wait for green and try again.

What does Stop and Go teach?

It gives children gentle, repeated practice pausing before acting, an early building block for impulse control.

Is Stop and Go free to play?

Yes. It plays free in any web browser with nothing to download or install.
