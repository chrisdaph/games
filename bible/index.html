Free Bible Games for Kids

Welcome to Bible Games for Kids, a growing collection of free, faith-based activities that bring favourite Bible stories to life. Right now you can play fifteen original jigsaw puzzles across three age groups and a Ten Commandments matching game — all in your browser, with nothing to download.

Puzzles for Every Age

  • Little Explorers — ages 2–5, big 4–6 piece puzzles.
  • Story Adventurers — ages 6–8, 9–16 piece puzzles.
  • Faith Builders — ages 9–12, 25–35 piece puzzles.
  • Ten Commandments Match — a matching game for all ages.

Perfect for Family Devotion & Sunday School

Each puzzle pairs a hands-on activity with a well-loved Bible story, making these games a simple way to open a conversation about faith at home, in the classroom, or during Sunday school. The artwork is original and gentle, and the controls work with a mouse or a finger on any touchscreen.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these Bible games free?

Yes. Every puzzle and game is completely free and plays in your web browser — no download, sign-up, or payment.

What ages are the Bible games for?

There are puzzles for ages 2–5, 6–8, and 9–12, plus a Ten Commandments matching game the whole family can enjoy.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Everything runs in the browser on phones, tablets, and computers, with mouse or touch controls.

Can I use these for Sunday school or homeschool?

Absolutely. The puzzles and matching game are great for Sunday school, homeschool lessons, and family devotion time.
