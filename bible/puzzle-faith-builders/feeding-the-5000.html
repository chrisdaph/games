About the Feeding the 5,000 Puzzle

This 35-piece jigsaw puzzle turns the Bible story of Feeding the 5,000 into a hands-on activity for ages 9-12. Every piece is original, colourful artwork with no photos, printing, or downloads needed, and the whole puzzle plays right in your web browser on phones, tablets, and computers.

The Story of Feeding the 5,000

A huge crowd had followed Jesus all day and grown hungry, but there was only a boy's small lunch of five loaves and two fish. Jesus gave thanks and began to share the food, and it kept multiplying. Everyone ate until they were full, with twelve baskets left over.

How to Play

  1. The mixed-up pieces wait in the trays on the left and right of the board.
  2. Drag a piece onto the spot where it belongs on the puzzle board.
  3. When a piece is in the right place it locks in with a satisfying snap.
  4. Keep going until every piece is placed and the Bible picture is complete!

What Kids Learn

  • Sharpens shape-matching, focus, and problem-solving
  • Builds hand-eye coordination with drag-and-drop or touch
  • Introduces the Bible story of Feeding the 5,000 (John 6:1-14)
  • Opens a natural conversation about how Jesus cares for people and God provides enough
