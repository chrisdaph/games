About the The First Christmas Puzzle

This 30-piece jigsaw puzzle turns the Bible story of The First Christmas into a hands-on activity for ages 9–12. Every piece is original, colourful artwork — no photos, printing, or downloads needed — and the whole puzzle plays right in your web browser on phones, tablets, and computers.

The Story of The First Christmas

On the first Christmas, Mary and Joseph travelled to Bethlehem, where baby Jesus was born in a humble stable. Angels announced the good news to shepherds, who hurried to see the newborn King. A bright star also guided wise men who came to bring Him gifts.

How to Play

  1. The mixed-up pieces wait in the trays on the left and right of the board.
  2. Drag a piece onto the spot where it belongs on the puzzle board.
  3. When a piece is in the right place it locks in with a satisfying snap.
  4. Keep going until every piece is placed and the Bible picture is complete!

What Kids Learn

  • Sharpens shape-matching, focus, and problem-solving
  • Builds hand-eye coordination with drag-and-drop or touch
  • Introduces the Bible story of The First Christmas (Luke 2:1–20)
  • Opens a natural conversation about the birth of Jesus and the first Christmas
