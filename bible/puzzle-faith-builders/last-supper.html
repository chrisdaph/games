About the The Last Supper Puzzle

This 30-piece jigsaw puzzle turns the Bible story of The Last Supper into a hands-on activity for ages 9–12. Every piece is original, colourful artwork — no photos, printing, or downloads needed — and the whole puzzle plays right in your web browser on phones, tablets, and computers.

The Story of The Last Supper

On the night before He died, Jesus shared a special meal with His closest friends. He broke bread and poured a cup, asking them to remember Him whenever they ate together. This Last Supper became a lasting picture of His love and sacrifice.

How to Play

  1. The mixed-up pieces wait in the trays on the left and right of the board.
  2. Drag a piece onto the spot where it belongs on the puzzle board.
  3. When a piece is in the right place it locks in with a satisfying snap.
  4. Keep going until every piece is placed and the Bible picture is complete!

What Kids Learn

  • Sharpens shape-matching, focus, and problem-solving
  • Builds hand-eye coordination with drag-and-drop or touch
  • Introduces the Bible story of The Last Supper (Matthew 26:17–30)
  • Opens a natural conversation about remembering Jesus' love and sacrifice
