About the Crossing the Red Sea Puzzle

This 25-piece jigsaw puzzle turns the Bible story of Crossing the Red Sea into a hands-on activity for ages 9–12. Every piece is original, colourful artwork — no photos, printing, or downloads needed — and the whole puzzle plays right in your web browser on phones, tablets, and computers.

The Story of Crossing the Red Sea

When the Israelites left Egypt, they came to the edge of the Red Sea with Pharaoh's army close behind. Moses stretched out his hand and God parted the waters, opening a dry path through the middle. The people crossed safely, and God protected them every step of the way.

How to Play

  1. The mixed-up pieces wait in the trays on the left and right of the board.
  2. Drag a piece onto the spot where it belongs on the puzzle board.
  3. When a piece is in the right place it locks in with a satisfying snap.
  4. Keep going until every piece is placed and the Bible picture is complete!

What Kids Learn

  • Sharpens shape-matching, focus, and problem-solving
  • Builds hand-eye coordination with drag-and-drop or touch
  • Introduces the Bible story of Crossing the Red Sea (Exodus 14)
  • Opens a natural conversation about how God makes a way and rescues His people
