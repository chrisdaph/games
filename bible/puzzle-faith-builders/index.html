About Faith Builders Bible Puzzles

Faith Builders is our most challenging set of Bible jigsaw puzzles, made for older children ages 9 to 12. With 25 to 35 pieces each, these detailed scenes reward patience and concentration while bringing well-loved Bible events to life.

Bible Stories in This Set

  • Crossing the Red Sea (Exodus 14) — a 25-piece puzzle about how God makes a way and rescues His people.
  • The First Christmas (Luke 2:1–20) — a 30-piece puzzle about the birth of Jesus and the first Christmas.
  • Feeding the 5,000 (John 6:1–14) — a 35-piece puzzle about how Jesus cares for people and God provides enough.
  • The Last Supper (Matthew 26:17–30) — a 30-piece puzzle about remembering Jesus' love and sacrifice.
  • The Empty Tomb (Matthew 28:1–10) — a 25-piece puzzle about the joy of Easter and the risen Jesus.

How to Play

  1. The mixed-up pieces wait in the trays on the left and right of the board.
  2. Drag a piece onto the spot where it belongs on the puzzle board.
  3. When a piece is in the right place it locks in with a satisfying snap.
  4. Keep going until every piece is placed and the Bible picture is complete!
