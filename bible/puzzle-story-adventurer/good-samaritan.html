About the The Good Samaritan Puzzle

This 12-piece jigsaw puzzle turns the Bible story of The Good Samaritan into a hands-on activity for ages 6–8. Every piece is original, colourful artwork — no photos, printing, or downloads needed — and the whole puzzle plays right in your web browser on phones, tablets, and computers.

The Story of The Good Samaritan

A traveler was hurt and left beside the road while others walked past without helping. Then a kind Samaritan stopped, cared for the man's wounds, and made sure he was looked after. Jesus told this story to teach us to be a good neighbour to everyone.

How to Play

  1. The mixed-up pieces wait in the trays on the left and right of the board.
  2. Drag a piece onto the spot where it belongs on the puzzle board.
  3. When a piece is in the right place it locks in with a satisfying snap.
  4. Keep going until every piece is placed and the Bible picture is complete!

What Kids Learn

  • Sharpens shape-matching, focus, and problem-solving
  • Builds hand-eye coordination with drag-and-drop or touch
  • Introduces the Bible story of The Good Samaritan (Luke 10:25–37)
  • Opens a natural conversation about kindness and being a good neighbour
