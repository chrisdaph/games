About the David and Goliath Puzzle

This 9-piece jigsaw puzzle turns the Bible story of David and Goliath into a hands-on activity for ages 6–8. Every piece is original, colourful artwork — no photos, printing, or downloads needed — and the whole puzzle plays right in your web browser on phones, tablets, and computers.

The Story of David and Goliath

Young David faced the giant Goliath with nothing but a sling, a few smooth stones, and a big faith in God. While others were afraid, David trusted that God would help him. With one brave throw, David won the day and showed that God gives courage to those who trust Him.

How to Play

  1. The mixed-up pieces wait in the trays on the left and right of the board.
  2. Drag a piece onto the spot where it belongs on the puzzle board.
  3. When a piece is in the right place it locks in with a satisfying snap.
  4. Keep going until every piece is placed and the Bible picture is complete!

What Kids Learn

  • Sharpens shape-matching, focus, and problem-solving
  • Builds hand-eye coordination with drag-and-drop or touch
  • Introduces the Bible story of David and Goliath (1 Samuel 17)
  • Opens a natural conversation about courage and trusting God in hard times
