About Story Adventurers Bible Puzzles

Story Adventurers is our middle set of Bible jigsaw puzzles, designed for early-elementary kids ages 6 to 8. With 9 to 16 pieces each, these puzzles offer just enough challenge to build focus and problem-solving while retelling exciting stories from the Bible.

Bible Stories in This Set

  • David and Goliath (1 Samuel 17) — a 9-piece puzzle about courage and trusting God in hard times.
  • Jonah and the Big Fish (Jonah 1–2) — a 12-piece puzzle about obedience, prayer, and second chances.
  • Daniel in the Lions' Den (Daniel 6) — a 16-piece puzzle about staying faithful and praying even when it's hard.
  • The Good Samaritan (Luke 10:25–37) — a 12-piece puzzle about kindness and being a good neighbour.
  • Zacchaeus in the Tree (Luke 19:1–10) — a 9-piece puzzle about how Jesus welcomes and changes everyone.

How to Play

  1. The mixed-up pieces wait in the trays on the left and right of the board.
  2. Drag a piece onto the spot where it belongs on the puzzle board.
  3. When a piece is in the right place it locks in with a satisfying snap.
  4. Keep going until every piece is placed and the Bible picture is complete!
