About the Zacchaeus in the Tree Puzzle

This 9-piece jigsaw puzzle turns the Bible story of Zacchaeus in the Tree into a hands-on activity for ages 6–8. Every piece is original, colourful artwork — no photos, printing, or downloads needed — and the whole puzzle plays right in your web browser on phones, tablets, and computers.

The Story of Zacchaeus in the Tree

Zacchaeus was a short tax collector who climbed a tree just to see Jesus in the crowd. Jesus looked up, called him by name, and came to his house. That day Zacchaeus changed his ways and promised to give back to those he had wronged.

How to Play

  1. The mixed-up pieces wait in the trays on the left and right of the board.
  2. Drag a piece onto the spot where it belongs on the puzzle board.
  3. When a piece is in the right place it locks in with a satisfying snap.
  4. Keep going until every piece is placed and the Bible picture is complete!

What Kids Learn

  • Sharpens shape-matching, focus, and problem-solving
  • Builds hand-eye coordination with drag-and-drop or touch
  • Introduces the Bible story of Zacchaeus in the Tree (Luke 19:1–10)
  • Opens a natural conversation about how Jesus welcomes and changes everyone
