About Little Explorers Bible Puzzles

Little Explorers is our set of the easiest Bible jigsaw puzzles, built for toddlers and preschoolers ages 2 to 5. Each puzzle uses only four to six large pieces with clear, friendly artwork, so young children can enjoy a sense of accomplishment while hearing a favourite Bible story.

Bible Stories in This Set

  • Noah's Ark (Genesis 6–9) — a 4-piece puzzle about God's protection and His rainbow promise.
  • Baby Moses in the Basket (Exodus 2:1–10) — a 6-piece puzzle about how God watched over Moses from the very start.
  • The Good Shepherd (John 10:11) — a 6-piece puzzle about Jesus as the shepherd who cares for us.
  • Jesus Blesses the Children (Mark 10:13–16) — a 6-piece puzzle about how much God loves children.
  • Jesus Calms the Storm (Mark 4:35–41) — a 4-piece puzzle about trusting Jesus when we feel afraid.

How to Play

  1. The mixed-up pieces wait in the trays on the left and right of the board.
  2. Drag a piece onto the spot where it belongs on the puzzle board.
  3. When a piece is in the right place it locks in with a satisfying snap.
  4. Keep going until every piece is placed and the Bible picture is complete!
