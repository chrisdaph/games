About the Jesus Calms the Storm Puzzle

This 4-piece jigsaw puzzle turns the Bible story of Jesus Calms the Storm into a hands-on activity for ages 2–5. Every piece is original, colourful artwork — no photos, printing, or downloads needed — and the whole puzzle plays right in your web browser on phones, tablets, and computers.

The Story of Jesus Calms the Storm

While Jesus and His friends were crossing the lake, a fierce storm began to rock the boat. The friends were frightened, but Jesus stood up and told the wind and waves to be still. At once everything grew calm, and the friends learned they could always trust Him.

How to Play

  1. The mixed-up pieces wait in the trays on the left and right of the board.
  2. Drag a piece onto the spot where it belongs on the puzzle board.
  3. When a piece is in the right place it locks in with a satisfying snap.
  4. Keep going until every piece is placed and the Bible picture is complete!

What Kids Learn

  • Sharpens shape-matching, focus, and problem-solving
  • Builds hand-eye coordination with drag-and-drop or touch
  • Introduces the Bible story of Jesus Calms the Storm (Mark 4:35–41)
  • Opens a natural conversation about trusting Jesus when we feel afraid
