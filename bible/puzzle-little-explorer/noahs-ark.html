About the Noah's Ark Puzzle

This 4-piece jigsaw puzzle turns the Bible story of Noah's Ark into a hands-on activity for ages 2–5. Every piece is original, colourful artwork — no photos, printing, or downloads needed — and the whole puzzle plays right in your web browser on phones, tablets, and computers.

The Story of Noah's Ark

In the story of Noah's Ark, God asked Noah to build a giant wooden boat and to bring two of every kind of animal on board. When the great flood came, everyone inside the ark stayed safe and dry. Afterward, God placed a colourful rainbow in the sky as a promise to always care for the world.

How to Play

  1. The mixed-up pieces wait in the trays on the left and right of the board.
  2. Drag a piece onto the spot where it belongs on the puzzle board.
  3. When a piece is in the right place it locks in with a satisfying snap.
  4. Keep going until every piece is placed and the Bible picture is complete!

What Kids Learn

  • Sharpens shape-matching, focus, and problem-solving
  • Builds hand-eye coordination with drag-and-drop or touch
  • Introduces the Bible story of Noah's Ark (Genesis 6–9)
  • Opens a natural conversation about God's protection and His rainbow promise
