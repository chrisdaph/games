About the The Good Shepherd Puzzle

This 6-piece jigsaw puzzle turns the Bible story of The Good Shepherd into a hands-on activity for ages 2–5. Every piece is original, colourful artwork — no photos, printing, or downloads needed — and the whole puzzle plays right in your web browser on phones, tablets, and computers.

The Story of The Good Shepherd

Jesus called Himself the Good Shepherd who lovingly looks after His sheep. A good shepherd knows each sheep by name, leads them to green grass, and keeps them safe from harm. This gentle picture reminds children that Jesus watches over them with care.

How to Play

  1. The mixed-up pieces wait in the trays on the left and right of the board.
  2. Drag a piece onto the spot where it belongs on the puzzle board.
  3. When a piece is in the right place it locks in with a satisfying snap.
  4. Keep going until every piece is placed and the Bible picture is complete!

What Kids Learn

  • Sharpens shape-matching, focus, and problem-solving
  • Builds hand-eye coordination with drag-and-drop or touch
  • Introduces the Bible story of The Good Shepherd (John 10:11)
  • Opens a natural conversation about Jesus as the shepherd who cares for us
