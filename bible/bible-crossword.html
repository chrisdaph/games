About the Bible Crossword

The Bible crossword is a simple, kid-friendly word puzzle that turns favourite Bible names and stories into a fun spelling game. Every clue comes with a picture emoji to help early readers, and answers are typed on a big on-screen keyboard, no typing skills or physical keyboard required. It plays free in any browser, great for Sunday school, homeschool, or family devotion time.

How to Play

  1. Pick a puzzle: Jesus, Noah, or Moses.
  2. Tap a clue in the list, or tap a square in the grid, to highlight a word.
  3. Tap letters on the keyboard to spell the answer. Correct letters lock in with a colour change.
  4. Keep going until every word in the puzzle is solved!

Great for Ages 5 and Up

Each puzzle uses short, simple words with picture clues, so kids who are just learning to read and spell can play along with a grown-up or older sibling. The on-screen keyboard means there is nothing to type by hand, just tap and go.
