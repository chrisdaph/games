About the Ten Commandments Match

The Ten Commandments matching game is a simple, screen-friendly way for kids to learn the ten rules God gave His people. Match each numbered stone tablet to a card that explains it in kid-friendly words, and see how quickly you can pair all ten. It plays free in any browser — great for Sunday school, homeschool, or family devotion time.

How to Play

  1. Tap a numbered stone tablet on the left.
  2. Tap the card on the right that matches what that commandment means.
  3. Correct matches lock in and the progress bar fills up.
  4. Keep matching until all ten commandments are paired!

The Ten Commandments for Kids

  1. Love God above everything else (Exodus 20:3)
  2. Don't worship idols or statues (Exodus 20:4–5)
  3. Use God's name with respect (Exodus 20:7)
  4. Rest and remember God on the Sabbath (Exodus 20:8–11)
  5. Honour your mother and father (Exodus 20:12)
  6. Do not hurt or harm others (Exodus 20:13)
  7. Be faithful and true in your family (Exodus 20:14)
  8. Do not steal (Exodus 20:15)
  9. Always tell the truth (Exodus 20:16)
  10. Be happy with what you have (Exodus 20:17)

Why Learn the Ten Commandments?

The Ten Commandments teach children how to love God and treat other people with kindness and respect. Turning them into a game helps the lessons stick, making them easy to remember and talk about at home or in class.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you play the Ten Commandments match?

Tap a stone tablet with a number, then tap the card that describes that commandment. Correct pairs lock in until you have matched all ten.

Is the Ten Commandments game free?

Yes. It plays free in any web browser with nothing to download or install.

Where are the Ten Commandments in the Bible?

God gave the Ten Commandments to Moses on Mount Sinai; you can read them in Exodus 20.

What age is this game for?

It is designed for all ages — a simple, friendly way for children and families to learn the Ten Commandments together.
