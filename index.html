Fun, Free Games for Kids

Kids Games is a home for simple, wholesome online activities that children can play on any phone, tablet, or computer. Everything runs right in your browser, there is nothing to download or install, and nothing to buy.

What You Can Play

Bible Games for Kids is our first collection: fifteen original jigsaw puzzles sorted into three age groups (2-5, 6-8, and 9-12) plus a Ten Commandments matching game for all ages. Toddler Games is a set of four games for ages 2-5 that build good behavior, patience, and feelings skills. More kid-friendly games are on the way.
